About Me

Two weeks after graduating from the University of Oregon College of Business with a Bachelor of Science in Accounting, I started full time in the public accounting profession with a large Oregon based firm.

After 11 positive and productive years with one firm, I decided a firm partnership wasn’t the direction I wanted to take my career. I wanted more face time with clients, not less. I no longer wanted billable hour budgets and profit margins to determine the success of a relationship. Lastly, I wanted to be strategic in my practice development. Growth for the sake of growth doesn’t motivate me. What does is sharing my nonprofit, healthcare, real estate and taxation expertise with the organizations and individuals that serve my community.

I am licensed by the Oregon Board of Accountancy to practice public accounting. My license number is 13176 and I am proud to have never had any form of disciplinary action against my license. You can search licensees and the status of disciplinary action with this link: http://licenseesearch.oregonboa.com/
